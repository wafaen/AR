Image Target → Looping Video AR

Static Kivicube-style workflow: compile your poster/image target, paste the hosted target + video links, then generate the AR viewer link.

1) Compile your target image

Upload the exact image people will scan. The app will create a .mind file. This is required for image tracking.

Download target.mind

Important: after download, upload target.mind somewhere public with HTTPS, then paste its direct URL below.

2) Build your AR link

Height ÷ width. A3 portrait is 1.414. Square is 1. Video will cover this full frame.

For GitHub Pages, this creates a link to viewer.html with your URLs inside the query string.

How to use it like Kivicube

  1. Upload the poster/image target here and compile it.
  2. Download target.mind.
  3. Upload target.mind and your video.mp4 to a public HTTPS host.
  4. Paste both direct URLs above.
  5. Open the generated viewer link on your phone and scan the original image.

Google Drive/Mega links often fail because they are not true direct/CORS-friendly links.
