Static WebAR · Image target video overlay

AR poster video builder

Paste a compiled MindAR image target and a direct MP4 URL. The viewer places the video exactly over the scanned image frame, with autoplay, mute, loop, and pause when the image is lost.

This is fully static: upload these files to GitHub Pages/Netlify. Your .mind and .mp4 links must be direct HTTPS links with CORS allowed.

Your poster/image must first be compiled using the MindAR Image Target Compiler.

Open viewer

How it works

  1. Use your poster/image as the image target.
  2. Compile it to targets.mind.
  3. Host targets.mind and the video online.
  4. Generate the link and put that link behind your QR code.
